Youth Workshops

Roslyn Code Club volunteers teach coding workshops at the Bryant Library the first Friday of each month. Workshops run from 3:45 to 5pm. Workshops are open to all students grades 4 through 8. Currently, we are teaching students how to use Scratch Jr. Please download the app on an iPad or Android tablet prior and bring it to the workshop. Sign-up on the Bryant Library website.
